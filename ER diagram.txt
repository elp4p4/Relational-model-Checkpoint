## Hotel Table:

**Primary Key:** 
Hotel_Id (INTEGER)

**Attributes:**
Hotel_Name (STRING)

## Employee Table:

**Primary Key:** 
Employee_Id (INTEGER)

**Attributes:**
Employee_Name (STRING)
Employee_Speciality (STRING)
Hotel_Id (INTEGER, Foreign Key referencing Hotel_Id in Hotel)
Leads_Employee_Id (INTEGER, Self-referencing Foreign Key)

## Type Table:

**Primary Key:** 
Type_Id (INTEGER)

**Attributes:**
Type_Name (STRING)

## Room Table:

**Primary Key:** 
Room_Id (INTEGER)

**Attributes:**
Floor (INTEGER)
Type_Id (INTEGER, Foreign Key referencing Type_Id in Type)
Hotel_Id (INTEGER, Foreign Key referencing Hotel_Id in Hotel)

## Category Table:

**Primary Key:** 
Category_Id (INTEGER)

**Attributes:**
Category_Name (STRING)
Price (DECIMAL)
Beds_Numbers (INTEGER)
